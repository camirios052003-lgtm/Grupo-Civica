# Vehicle Maintenance Schedule

## 📅 Regular Maintenance Intervals

### Every 5,000 km (3,000 miles)
- ✅ Engine oil & filter change
- ✅ Tire pressure check & rotation
- ✅ Visual brake inspection

### Every 15,000 km (10,000 miles)
- ✅ Air filter replacement
- ✅ Cabin air filter replacement
- ✅ Brake fluid level check
- ✅ Battery terminals inspection
- ✅ Wiper blades inspection

### Every 30,000 km (20,000 miles)
- ✅ Brake pads inspection/replacement
- ✅ Transmission fluid check
- ✅ Coolant level check
- ✅ Spark plugs inspection (gasoline engines)
- ✅ Drive belt inspection

### Every 50,000 km (30,000 miles)
- ✅ Transmission fluid change
- ✅ Coolant flush & replace
- ✅ Spark plugs replacement
- ✅ Fuel filter replacement
- ✅ Power steering fluid check

### Every 100,000 km (60,000 miles)
- ✅ Timing belt/chain inspection
- ✅ Water pump inspection
- ✅ Full suspension check
- ✅ Exhaust system inspection
- ✅ Major brake service (rotors + pads)

## ⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

1. **Check Engine Light** — Get diagnostic scan immediately
2. **Unusual noises** — Grinding, squealing, clicking, knocking
3. **Fluid leaks** — Oil (brown/black), Coolant (green/orange), Transmission (red)
4. **Vibrations** — While driving, braking, or idling
5. **Smoke from exhaust** — White (coolant), Blue (oil), Black (fuel)
6. **Reduced performance** — Poor acceleration, rough idle, stalling

## 🌡️ Seasonal Tips (Colombia / Tropical Climates)

- **Rainy season**: Check wiper blades, tire tread depth, and brake condition
- **Hot weather**: Monitor coolant levels, battery condition, and tire pressure
- **High altitude (Bogotá 2,640m)**: Engine may need altitude adjustment, check air filter more frequently
- **Coastal cities (Cartagena, Santa Marta)**: Watch for salt corrosion on undercarriage
